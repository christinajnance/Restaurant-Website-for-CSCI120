Welcome

What's up my fellow food-loving friends? I would like to officially welcome you to Christina's Food Fusion, a newly renovated restaurant here in Conway, SC. Thank you for visiting our site. It is my hope that you will find all the information you need here such as our mission, history, and more. To learn more about this restaurant, please click here.

Here at Christin's Food Fusion (CFF) we do it a little differently than other restaurants you might go to. C's Kitchen is a restaurant that serves Christina's favorite recipes from fried chicken to hibachi. Yes ma'am. We are serving it all. Come get you some today!

History

In highschool, Christina was in the Science and Technology Program where she fell in love with Computer Science and coding. As a result, she decided to pursue a dual degree in Computer Science and Information Technology to learn the technical and cyber security side of computers. She aspires to one work for the FBI in their cyber crime division as a computer forensics analyst.

In her first semester at Coastal Carolina University, she took Interface to Web Interface Development where she created this webpage with her favorite foods. If you know Christina, you know she loves to eat so this page suites her well. From food lover to fellow food lover, we hope this page fits your need and satisfies your desires. Mission

Our mission at Christina's Food Fusion is to provide of a variety of food options that cover a majority of the food genres in order to serve as a one stop shop for all kinds of cravings one may have when hungry. We hope to fuel your body with healthier options of foodthan our counterparts. We strive to use whole wheat products and make each dish from scratch. Therefore, our servers and customers know exactly whats in the food we serve.

On top of that, our restaurant holds special events every month where customers can get discounts on meals the day of the event as well as in the near future. To learn more about these events, please click here.
