Special Events

"BOGO" Fridays

"BOGO" Fridays is an event where you can buy one meal and get one for free so if you want to have Christina's Food Fusion (CFF) for lunch and dinner or want a cheaper dinner date with twice the quality. Christina's Food Fusion is the restaurant for you. "BOGO" Fridays also allows you to build your own meal with a side of chips and fountain drink.

Weekly Smoothies

CFF wants to add a smoothie menu therefore each week, CFF will have a smoothie of the week. If a customer buys that smoothie, the smoothie is 1/2 off. Any smoothie you buy that isn't the weekly smoothie will get a card punch. The top 5 smoothies with the most sales during a 6 month period will be added to the menu permanently.

C's Gift to You

Upon ordering our restaurant gift card, you'll get a free item every month. The item will only be free during that month. In order to be eligible for the free item requires consistent purchases of food items here at CFF.
